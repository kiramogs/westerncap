About Us

Our vision is to be a niche player in the financial services segment and help NBFC/Fintech/SMEs in their growth capital and product offerings to get better return of equity to partners. We aim to be the most reliable player with best industry practices and transparent transactions.
Now and Future
The company was founded in 2019 with a vision to support the financial services ecosystem by providing growth capital to upcoming players. We started with funding NBFC/Fintech/MFI/SME customers for their growth capital. Later, we found the opportunity to support the new age companies through partnership model. This will help our partners to utilize their resources in a more effective way. We offer solutions for supply chain finance, unsecured loans, personal loans & two wheeler loans. We also have one of the best lending software in the industry where we offer a 100% digital journey to our customers. The company has also seen immense growth with a growth rate of almost 100% from the year of establishment and is set to increase further in the current financial year.

About our Founder

Mr. Anil Kejriwal - Founder of Western Capital

Western Capital is the brain child of Mr. Anil Kumar Kejriwal, who has the passion to strive for the best in his businesses. This was proven from his other business ventures in India & Abroad. He has done his Bachelor's Degree in Finance from Sonoma University, California and an Expert in Domestic & International Manufacturing & Trade. He started his business as a small NBFC at Jharkand in early 2000's and expanded his business empires to Africa, Europe & Middle East over the past 13 years.

In 2016 Mr. Anil Kejriwal found the need of Digitalization in the Indian Lending space and started his ground work on starting up a NBFC to serve the underserved segment of Indian lending Space. Western Capital got a licence in Jan 2019 to start the operations. Mr. Anil Kejriwal found the Niche of serving the small NBFC/Fintech to their growth capital as these segments are facing lot of credit crunch due to the recent change in approach by the RBI/Banks
